Vegetation stress & health from NDVI patterns

Question: Where and when does vegetation show signs of stress or high health based on NDVI patterns?

Home Exploratory visualizations

Data & methods

Monthly composites use MODIS Terra L3 NDVI Monthly from NASA GIBS WMS (Aqua is used only for April 2025 when Terra has no tile). Pixels are decoded with the official GIBS NDVI colormap to produce approximate regional means (exploratory / visualization-scale, not raw science retrieval).

  • Regions: California Central Valley, Pacific Northwest, Great Plains, Southeast US, Amazon Basin.
  • Stress share: share of vegetated pixels with NDVI below 0.30.
  • High-health share: share with NDVI at or above 0.60.

Interactive visualization

Pick a region and month to see MODIS NDVI from NASA GIBS alongside the same regional mean NDVI, stress share, and high-health share. For static exploratory charts (time series, distributions, heatmap, etc.), see Exploratory visualizations.
