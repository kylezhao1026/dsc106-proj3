Interactive MODIS NDVI & vegetation stress

Data from data/modis_ndvi_region_month.json. Use View to show one region or compare two side by side; use the trend controls or month scrubber for the selected year. Map tiles: NASA GIBS WMS MODIS_Terra_L3_NDVI_Monthly; maps use MODIS_Aqua_L3_NDVI_Monthly only for 2025-04-01 (Terra tile missing that month on GIBS). Stress = share of pixels with NDVI < 0.30 (decoded colormap); high health = NDVI ≥ 0.60.

Open this page via a local server (browsers block fetch from file://): python3 -m http.server 8080 then visit http://localhost:8080/interactive.html

Choose two different regions to compare.

Drag or tap the scrubber to pick a month!
